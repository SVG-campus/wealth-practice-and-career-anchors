To serve Ultra-High-Net-Worth Individuals (UHNWI) and Single/Multi-Family Offices (SFO/MFO) across California and New York, you maintain a dual-state licensing structure:

## 3. High-Value Product Offerings Breakdown

### A. Premium Financed Life Insurance for SFOs
- **Mechanics:** A third-party commercial bank lends the capital required to fund high-cash-value life insurance policies for family office patriarchs/matriarchs.
- **Benefit:** Preserves working capital in live investments (or trading algos) while accumulating millions in tax-advantaged cash value.

### B. Irrevocable Life Insurance Trusts (ILITs) with AetherQuant Risk Overlay
- **Mechanics:** The policy is owned by an independent ILIT for the benefit of heirs.
- **Benefit:** Death benefit proceeds are excluded from the gross estate, providing 100% tax-free liquidity to cover federal estate taxes (which apply at 40% over exemption limits).
- **Proprietary Edge:** Offering AetherQuant's +12%/day algorithmic trading strategy as a sub-advised hedging overlay for cash values within variable products or associated family trusts.
